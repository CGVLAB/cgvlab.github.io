The Computer Graphics and Visualization Laboratory (CGVLab) is an academic research lab that is part of the Department of Computer Science at Purdue University. The group conducts research in graphics, visualization, computational geometry, and related applications. Focus areas include model acquisition, image generalization, scientific visualization, urban modeling, robust computational geometry, and geometric computations and constraints.
